# `TextArea`

Multi-line text field based on a native `<textarea>`.

## Use when

- longer free text should be collected

## Important props

- `name`
- `label`
- `value`
- `onChange`
- `rows`
- `required`
- `error`

## Notes

- Validation and error rendering work through `FieldWrapper`, just like the other fields.
- The field is intentionally simple and relies on native browser behavior.
